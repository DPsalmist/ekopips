Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our trading programs, learning process, and mentorship.

Do I need prior trading experience to join?

No, you don’t need any prior experience. Our Beginner program is designed to start from the basics and gradually build your knowledge and skills.

How long does each program take to complete?

Each level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced) is structured to run for a few weeks with flexible learning. You can learn at your own pace, but most learners complete a program in 4–8 weeks.

Will I get practical trading experience?

Yes! We use demo accounts and real market scenarios to help you practice safely before moving to live trading. Mentors guide you through the process.

Do you provide mentorship and support after training?

Absolutely! We don’t stop at teaching — you’ll have access to mentors, updates, and our trading community to continue growing even after completing the program.

How much does it cost to join?

We offer flexible pricing depending on the program level. Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced programs each have different rates. You can check details in the Programs section.
